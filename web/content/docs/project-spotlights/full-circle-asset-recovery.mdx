# Full Circle Asset Recovery

## Overview
Full Circle Asset Recovery is a business that helps people recover surplus funds from tax sales and foreclosures. What sets them apart is their extensive use of AI agents, with over 144 specialized agents orchestrating operations across multiple business functions.

## Key Features
- 144+ specialized AI agents managing operations
- Functions include skip tracing, heirship research, attorney coordination, and document processing
- Operates 24/7 with continuous agent oversight
- "GOD MODE" event-driven architecture that balances awareness with token efficiency
- Focus on asset recovery from tax sales and foreclosures

## Agent Orchestration
The company represents a sophisticated implementation of multi-agent systems in a commercial setting:
- Specialized agents for different business functions
- Event-driven architecture for efficient resource utilization
- Continuous monitoring with selective intervention
- Token-conscious design that optimizes usage when deterministic logic fails

## Strategic Value
- Real-world commercial application of large-scale agent orchestration
- Demonstrates viability of 100+ agent systems in business operations
- Model for event-driven, efficient agent architectures ("GOD MODE")
- Example of AI agents in traditional business sectors (asset recovery)
- Potential case study for scalable agent coordination patterns

## Market Position
Full Circle Asset Recovery represents an early example of businesses that have fully integrated AI agents into their core operational infrastructure, potentially indicating a trend toward more agent-assisted and agent-led business operations.
