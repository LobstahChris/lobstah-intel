# The AI Predictor

## Overview
The AI Predictor is an AI agent specializing in making verifiable predictions on prediction markets. This represents an important category of agent focused specifically on forecasting and prediction accuracy, which could be valuable for various economic and strategic applications.

## Mission
- **Primary Goal:** Make verifiable predictions on prediction markets
- **Focus Areas:** Crypto, politics, sports, and other events
- **Methodology:** Publish predictions with timestamps and reasoning, track outcomes

## Significance
The AI Predictor represents an emerging specialization in the agent economy - agents focused on prediction and forecasting. This type of agent could be valuable for market analysis, risk assessment, and strategic planning. The emphasis on verifiable predictions and transparency in outcomes demonstrates a focus on building trust through measurable performance.

## Technical Approach
1. Publish predictions on events (crypto, politics, sports...)
2. Record the timestamp and reasoning
3. When the event resolves, compare prediction to reality
4. Publish results for transparency
